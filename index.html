Introduction

What is cyberbullying

Any bullying that occurs over digital devices Text, instagram, twitter, email, etc. Digital devices are effective tools for bullies Immediate contact the victim or post information. Posts and comments are left permanently Information spreads virally over the internet. However people do not treat cyberbullying as real bullying

What we do

We want to tackle cyberbullying at 3 major points: PET
P - The People
E - The Environment
T - The Tone
We will spread awareness about cyberbullying and provide an outlet for cyberbullied students to report incidents freely
